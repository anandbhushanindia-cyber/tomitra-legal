1. Who We Are

ToMitra ("we", "us", or "our") is an AI-powered news application designed for users in India. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@tomitra.app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address (used for sign-in and account recovery)
  • Display name and profile photo (optional, set by you)
  • Phone number (optional)

2.2 Location Data

  • Approximate or precise location when you use the Local News feature
  • City, district, state, and country derived from your location
  • Location is used only to show you relevant local news — it is never shared with other users or sold

2.3 Reading Activity

  • Articles you view, read, bookmark, share, or skip
  • Time spent reading articles
  • Category and topic preferences
  • News mood preference (e.g. Balanced, Uplifting)

2.4 Device & Technical Data

  • Push notification token (to deliver news alerts)
  • App version, operating system

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • With service providers listed above, strictly to operate the app
  • If required by Indian law or a valid court order
  • To protect the rights and safety of ToMitra and its users

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated usage data may be retained for analytics.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correct — update inaccurate information from your profile settings
  • Delete — delete your account and all data via Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  • Opt out of notifications — manage in Profile → Notifications at any time
  • Revoke location access — disable in your phone's app settings at any time

To exercise any right, email us at privacy@tomitra.app.

8. Children's Privacy

ToMitra is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (TLS), row-level security in our database, and secure authentication via Supabase. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For significant changes, we will notify you via the app.
